如何安裝 https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jUZZC9UXyFs
創一個src file
把 SDL2-devel-2.30.3-mingw /i686-w64/的 include,lib 複製到src 
把 bin/SDL2.dll 也複製到資料夾下
編譯	
g++ -I src/include -L src/lib -o main main.cpp -lmingw32 -lSDL2main -lSDL2



g++ -o main main.cpp mygamesrc/src/GameLogic.cpp mygamesrc/src/EventHandler.cpp mygamesrc/src/Rendering.cpp -Imygamesrc/include -Isrc/include -Lsrc/lib -lmingw32 -lSDL2main -lSDL2
